About Cowboy Safari

Cowboy Safari – Cowboy Adventure, Animal Taming & Sky Zoo Building

Cowboy Safari is an endless runner game that combines thrilling action, animal taming, and zoo management. Players take on the role of a brave cowboy, lassoing and riding wild animals from buffaloes and zebras to elephants and lions, and building a unique Sky Zoo in the sky. Every adventure is full of excitement, requiring precision, timing, and quick reflexes.

How to Play Cowboy Safari: Ride, Tame, and Rule

In Cowboy Safari, you become a courageous cowboy, adventure through wild terrains, lasso and ride animals to conquer challenges, and expand your Sky Zoo.

Start the Adventure

  • Hold Spacebar to jump and lasso animals.
  • Use arrow keys to dodge obstacles and steer your cowboy.

Tame Animals

Watch each animal’s anger meter; jump and ride at the right time to avoid being thrown off.

Unique traits per animal:

  • Buffalo: Easy to control, strong
  • Zebra: Fast but unpredictable
  • Ostrich: Great at smashing crates when upgraded
  • Elephant: Durable but slow turning
  • Lion: Aggressive, for advanced players

Manage Your Sky Zoo

Transfer tamed animals to the zoo.

Upgrade habitats to:

  • Extend ride time before animals get angry
  • Increase income from visitors
  • Unlock special abilities and rare species

Complete Missions

Each area offers missions with gold rewards:

Boss Missions: Special challenges with tougher terrain and rare animals; upgrade your animals to at least level 4–5 before attempting.

Animal & Game Controls

  • Lasso Animal:  Spacebar
  • Hold Lasso on Animal: Hold Spacebar
  • Jump to Next Animal: Spacebar near animal
  • Move Left/Right: Arrow keys
  • Navigate Cowboy: Arrow Up/Down

Gameplay Tips for Mastering Cowboy Safari

Time Animal Anger Properly. Watch each animal’s anger meter. Jump or ride at the right moment to avoid being thrown off and maintain momentum throughout the adventure.

Jump Only When in Lasso Range. Avoid jumping too early; only jump when you are close enough to successfully lasso and ride the animal.

Prioritize New Animal Species. Taming new animals increases long-term income for your Sky Zoo and unlocks special features.

Upgrade Habitats Strategically. Focus on upgrading each habitat to level 4 first. This extends ride duration, boosts income, and creates a foundation for upgrading animals later.

Collect Bonus Crates Wisely. Some animals gain bonus gold from crates when upgraded. Plan your path to maximize crate rewards efficiently.

Plan Your Next Animal Carefully.When preparing to jump, choose animals that are less angry or can run far, helping you extend distance and earn more gold..
